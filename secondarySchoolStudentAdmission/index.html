Brief History of the school
OGS School, Offa, Kwara (Day and Secondary school), is a very successful and flourishing co-educational secondary school in the heart of

Kwara. it has over 450 students. We are academically selective and highly so, with everyone being above the average.
From the very first moment a child enters the school, they will benefit from an excellent academic

education supported by highly qualified and experienced specialist staff and a large range of musical,

creative, sporting and artistic opportunities which are essential for children’s all-round education and

development. The care and welfare of the students is of paramount importance and we make every

effort to ensure that each student feels secure and happy. Our ethos is founded on discipline and

encouragement within a traditional framework.

History

Founded in September 1960 by Arch. Patrick Anyaene and late Lady Uche Rosarii Anyaene, Offa Grammar College has a long history of delivering top class education.

Late Lady Uche Anyaene also doubled as the Proprietress and pioneer Principal of the school.
